In today’s dynamic cyber landscape, evolving and thriving requires a proactive approach to security. Join us for an engaging and comprehensive workshop designed to elevate your security posture with Microsoft 365. This workshop will provide you with the tools, knowledge, and hands-on experience needed to enhance your organisation’s security and efficiency.

Key Topics Covered:

* Privileged Identity Management (PIM): Discover governance strategies and setup tips to enhance your security posture. We will guide you step-by-step through setting up your own PIM model, governance, and workflow using our detailed template. This session will ensure you have a robust framework to manage and monitor privileged access effectively.

* PowerShell & Automation: Unlock the potential of automation to streamline your administrative tasks. Learn how to leverage dynamic groups for use in automation, PIM, and other daily administrative tasks. This segment will empower you to automate repetitive tasks, saving time and reducing the risk of human error.

* Security Optimization Assessments (SOA): Learn to audit your environment using Microsoft’s free SOA PowerShell scripts. This session will provide you with the skills to conduct thorough security assessments, identify vulnerabilities, and implement necessary improvements to fortify your defences.

* Exchange Online Protection (EOP): Get guidelines to configure and optimise your EOP settings. This will leverage information gathered in the SOA audit to ensure your email environment is secure and resilient against threats. We will cover best practices and advanced configurations to maximise your protection.

* Phishing Simulation: Utilise Microsoft Defender tools to simulate and mitigate phishing attacks effectively. This session will include using the built-in training resources for your staff, ensuring they are well-prepared to recognise and respond to phishing attempts. We will also share tips, tricks, and common pitfalls to avoid when using these tools.

Equip yourself with the knowledge and skills to not just survive but thrive in the ever-changing digital landscape. This workshop is your opportunity to stay ahead of the curve, ensuring your organisation is secure, efficient, and ready to face any cyber challenge.
